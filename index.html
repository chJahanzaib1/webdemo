About Us

Goddard-Discovery is an incubating startup focused on leveraging Machine Learning tools and techniques to optimize R&D work. Through Machine Learning based discovery assistant, Goddard-Discovery enable researchers to organize, retrieve, and formulate intra disciplinary research ideas efficiently and conveniently.We envision to boost scientific discoveries and innovations in every domain.
